About Us

The Purpose of Our Website

The website provides a detailed summary of livestock diseases, complete with descriptions, symptoms, potential cures, and associated vaccines. Farmers can easily identify symptoms of common diseases and access treatment options or preventive measures. This system empowers farmers to act quickly when signs of an illness appear, minimizing animal loss and ensuring the well-being of livestock.

Vaccination Information

It also offers information on various livestock vaccinations (e.g., internal/external). Farmers can learn about the different types of vaccines available, the diseases they prevent, and the recommended vaccination schedules. These vaccines may also be useful for preventing and dealing with various parasites, further ensuring the well-being of livestock.

Animal Health Tracking

The system includes an animal health-tracking feature, which allows farmers to track the health history of their livestock. This is particularly useful for managing large herds and ensuring the health of animals, especially in rural or expansive areas.

Breeding and Productivity

The system tracks the productivity of the livestock, including the number of animals that were able to produce offspring. It keeps records of the offspring number and continues monitoring them like the rest of the livestock, ensuring the well-being of the herds.
