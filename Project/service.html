Lumpy Skin Disease

A viral disease affecting cattle and buffalo, causing skin nodules, fever, and weight loss. It spreads through insect bites and can lead to severe economic losses due to reduced milk production and fertility issues.

Brucellosis

A bacterial infection that affects cattle, goats, and sheep. It causes reproductive issues like abortion in females and can spread to humans through unpasteurized dairy. Brucellosis can lead to long-term herd health problems.

Discovered on: 2024-08-01

Anthrax

A severe bacterial disease affecting cattle, goats, and sheep. It causes sudden death, bleeding from orifices, and can spread rapidly. Anthrax spores can live in soil for decades, posing an ongoing risk to livestock and humans.
